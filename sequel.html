The Adventure in the Haunted House

Kenny and Joseph finally escape the haunted house safely after being trap there for more than a month. As they walk back to their town, something doesn't feel right. The whole town is deserted. No one is there. But on one night, all the town members walk back into the town from the woods. However, these town members act very weird. Out of curiosity and the will to save their loved ones, Kenny and Joseph set out to find the truth behind all this. Join Kenny and Joseph to discover the truth.

  • Charles Brady as Joseph
  • William Campbell as town member one
  • Thomas Carson as town member two
  • David Dickinson as town member three
